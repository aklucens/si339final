About Anna

Family

Family History with Cultural Exploration

I grew up in a household where cultural exploration was a prioritization. With my mom's background as a child of Chinese immigrants, I found myself immersed in Asian culture. With my dad's background as a child of Lithuanian immigrants, I grew up with a unique perspective on an Eastern European heritage.

Passion

Drive for Cultural Curiosity

Fostering this sense of cultural awareness won’t come easy, but turning the challenges into opportunities for continued learning will be the most rewarding experience. I will continue my cultural curiosity, from questions about the best traditional food to cultural traditions and customs to follow. I want to make the most of my experience in this program, challenging myself to live in the moment, value perseverance, and be courageous in all of my international endeavors.

Experience

Having explored almost all 50 states and countries across Europe, North America, and South America, I am determined to continue exploring a wide variety of desinations.
